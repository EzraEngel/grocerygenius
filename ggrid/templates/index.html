Welcome

This is a prototype interface for a new way to shop for groceries. Please read the instructions below before continuing as data will be analyzed to make further improvements to the design.
Instructions.The goal of this exercise is to browse through the inventory in the same way that you might do in a phyiscal store. You can can navigate and build your cart using the commands below. Try to move through the mock storefront deliberately and quickly, so that we get a good sense of how long navigation takes under realistic conditions. When you complete browsing, you'll be prompted to complete a survey about your experience.
Keyboard Controls
  • Arrow Keys: Navigate between items
  • Mouse Click: Select and add item to cart
  • Space: Add selected item to cart
  • Backspace: Remove selected item to cart
  • Escape: Switch to cart navigation
  • Tab (+Shift): Go to next (previous) shelf
  • Enter (+Shift): Go to next (previous) section

Participant Consent. Your participation in this study is completely voluntary, and you are free to leave at any time. All data that we collect from your trial here will be completely anonymous, and will be aggregated to determine overall usage trends and areas for interface improvement. Please hit the Enter button below and enjoy the site if you're cool with all of that.
